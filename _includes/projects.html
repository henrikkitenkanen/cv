Projects

A list of scientific research projects / groups where I have been working.

Digital Geography Lab - Digital Geography Lab at the University of Helsinki.
Accessibility Research Group - Analyzing spatial accessibility and mobility using novel data sources.
Social media data for Conservation Science -project - In this project, the aim is to understand if and how geospatial social media data could be used to support conservation science and practice.
Automating GIS processes -course - I have developed an openly available online course for learning to automatize various GIS-related tasks and analyses using Python programming language. The teaching utilizes cloud computing (CSC cPouta), GitHub Classrooms, and Sphinx for writing the online materials.
